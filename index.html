JIRAN CERAMICS

寂然: 'serene stillness'

寂然 (Jiran) ,a.k.a. Jessie, is a Brooklyn-based ceramic and sculptural artist with roots originating from Beijing, China.

Her practice is inspired by her Chinese name, which stands for serene stillness, drawing sense of nature, organic matters, and mindfulness to every spin of the wheel.



Jiran Ceramics' mission is to create beautiful, functional art that enhances your home and life.

Est. October 2022
Brooklyn, New York
